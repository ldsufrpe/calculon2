Resposta 1.19.1.
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial w}{\partial x} = 2 \, x y \ln\left(x z\right) + x y \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial w}{\partial y} = x^{2} \ln\left(x z\right) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{\partial w}{\partial z}= \frac{x^{2} y}{z} \end{equation*}
